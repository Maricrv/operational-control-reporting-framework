# Process Risks and Controls
This document summarizes **key operational risks** by process stage and the **controls used to mitigate them**.

---

## Preparation Phase
| Risk | Control |
|---|---|
| Incomplete readiness | Readiness checklist and sign-off |
| Resource gaps | Coverage validation and backup planning |
| Reporting not tested | Pre-operation reporting validation |

---

## Execution Phase
| Risk | Control |
|---|---|
| Schedule deviation | Progress tracking vs expected curve |
| Site not reporting | Reporting compliance KPI and fallback channels |
| Incident accumulation | Incident SLA monitoring and escalation rules |
| Data inconsistencies | Ongoing reconciliation checks |

---

## Closure Phase
| Risk | Control |
|---|---|
| Incomplete processing | Completion rate validation |
| Late data errors | Final reconciliation checks |
| Unresolved critical incidents | Closure approval gate |
