# Stakeholders and Information Needs

This section identifies the key stakeholders involved in the operation and outlines their primary information needs across different stages of the operational lifecycle.

---

## Central Operations Management
Responsible for overall operational oversight and decision-making across all regions.

## Regional Operational Leads
Coordinate activities at the regional or site level.

## Support and Incident Management Teams
Provide operational, technical, and incident support.

## External Service Providers
Deliver contracted infrastructure and services.

## Compliance, Audit, and Oversight Bodies
Ensure regulatory compliance and transparency.

## Senior Leadership
Accountable for overall success and credibility of the operation.
